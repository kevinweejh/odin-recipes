Best Ever Shrimp Dip

Description

This creamy shrimp dip is great with chips, crackers or bread. It's always the first thing to go at any party!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • ½ pound cooked and peeled shrimp

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese and mayonnaise. Mix in onion, celery and shrimp meat. Cover bowl, and refrigerate before serving.
